DEMOGRAPHICS

Data collection took place between 2 and 31 July using household survey methodology applied to a simple random sample of households in 31 refugee settlements in Cox Bazar District. A total of 3,171 interviews were completed, gender balanced with 51% male respondents and 49% female. Conducted towards the end of monsoon season, this assessment provides data that is statistically representative at the camp level and for the response as a whole.
